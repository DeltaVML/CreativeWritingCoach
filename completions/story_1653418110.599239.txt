The opening of this story is very strong, and immediately draws the reader in with a sense of mystery. The first paragraph does a great job of worldbuilding and setting the scene for the story. However, the second paragraph feels a little bit exposition-heavy, and could be trimmed down to improve the pacing of the story.

The action scenes in this story are well written and easy to follow. The use of spells and magical jargon is well done and adds to the worldbuilding. However, there are a few places where the story could be tightened up, such as in the third paragraph, where there is a lot of telling instead of showing.
